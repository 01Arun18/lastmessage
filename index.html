A final letter

have written every line with a meaning
janichi bahut thare emiti heichi ki tate lagithiba arun kn paen mate emiti kahuchi emiti kn paen treat karuchi... jadi moh hathre ebe se dina thanta tah mu karinathanti, tate se dukha ki kasta deinathanti, heipare tora choto choto icha b puura kariba paen chesta karithanti... bhabisya bisya re na bhabiki tate se moment re khusi deithanti
moh mana re sabu bele toh katha hi aase sabu bele tu hi aasu... bahut ekla heijaichi isha tu gala pare bahut weak heigali... kebe bhabinathili toh bina rahibaku pdiba mate bahut ekla heijaichi toh bina
mu tate hajeiki janili ki tate mu kete bhala pauthili, bas kebe kahi parlini... tate gote "i love you" kahiba paen bhabuthili ki jadi tate sabu bele kahibi tah tate kebe tah value feel habani... bas bhabuthili ki future re kebe bahut bda surprise deiki kahibi
kebe bhabuna moh luha ra badla tate bhagwan debe... tu daba kasta paen kanduni, aame alga haba dukha re kaduchi, tate khusi re na rakhi pariba dukha re kanduchi
bas etiki kahibi last re... bahut nijara thilu tu, bahut adhika bhala pauthili, moh dhana thilu tu... mu sabu kichi bhuliba paen raji thili, nua aarambha kariba paen, bahut aasa thila ki sabu kichi thik heijiba... tate bahut adhika khusi re rakhibi... kebe aau dukha debini bahut aasa re thili
bahut kichi rakhi deithili nija bhitre, nija mana re... kebe aau kariparlini... kahidauchi ebe isha, bahut bhala pauthili, tu mora favourite thilu, i loved you a lot tah bina bahut ekla heijaichi... mate bahut regret hue ae katha ra ki tate mu jetiki khusi deichi tah thu adhika dukha deli

some things are complete, not forgotten
